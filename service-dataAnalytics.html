Where analytics projects usually go wrong

Dashboards get rebuilt repeatedly because KPI definitions are inconsistent, source logic keeps changing, and different teams report the same thing in different ways.

  • Dashboard redesign for leadership reporting
  • KPI and metric definition workshops
  • ETL and reporting workflow review
  • Basic governance for recurring reports
